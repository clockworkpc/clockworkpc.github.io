## Text Objects

| Shortcut   | Meaning                 |
|------------|-------------------------|
| `iw`       | inner word              |
| `it`       | inner tag (HTML)        |
| `i"`, `i'` | inner quotes, inclusive |
| `ip`       | inner paragraph         |
| `as`       | a sentence              |

hello this as a sentence with lots of words and the wordsomething

and here is another sentence with the word other
